Hazrat Hussain (Ra)

Name :

Hussain ibn e Ali RA

Title :

Nawas e Rasool

Father :

Ali ibn e Talib RA (cousin of Prophet ﷺ)

Mother :

Fatima bint e Mohammad ﷺ

Birth :

4th Sha’ban, 4 AH, Madinah

Death & Cause:

Shaheed, 10 Moharram, in Karbala, Iraq

Grew up under the direct guidance of the Prophet ﷺ.

Known for piety, generosity, courage, and love for justice.

The Prophet ﷺ loved Hassan (RA) and Hussain (RA) deeply and referred to them as “the leaders of the youth of Paradise.”
