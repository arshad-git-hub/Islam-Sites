Seerat : Hazrat Ali (Ra)

Name :

Ali ibn e Talib RA

Title :

Asadullah. Murtaza, Haidar, Abu Turab

Father :

Abu Talib RA(uncle of Prophet ﷺ)

Mother :

Fatima bint Asad (RA)

Birth :

13 Rajab, 600 CE (approx.) in the Kaaba, Makkah

Death & Cause:

Shaheed, 19 Ramadan 40 AH (661 CE)

One of the earliest converts to Islam (at age ~10)

Known for his courage, wisdom, and deep knowledge

Slept in the Prophet’s bed during Hijrah (migration to Madinah) to protect him from Quraysh assassins

Participated in all major battles of Islam except the Battle of Tabuk

Married to Hazrat Fatimah (RA), daughter of Prophet ﷺ

Children: Hasan (RA), Husain (RA), Zainab (RA), Umm Kulthum (RA)

Became the 4th Caliph of Islam after Hazrat Uthman (RA) in 656 CE

His caliphate lasted about 5 years (656–661 CE)

Faced internal conflicts: Battle of Jamal, Battle of Siffin, Battle of Nahrawan

Established justice, simplicity, and equality in governance

Known for Nahj al-Balagha, a collection of his sermons, letters, and sayings

Famous for bravery, piety, and deep understanding of Qur’an

Known for wise judgments and fairness as chief judge (Qaazi) during earlier caliphs

Attacked by Abdur-Rahman ibn Muljam (a Kharijite) with a poisoned sword during Fajr prayer

Passed away on 21st Ramadan on 19 Ramadan 40 AH (661 CE) in Kufa, Iraq
